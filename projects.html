Home Projects Home Networking Renovation Project

Over this previous summer, I completely re-did the network infrastructure at my home to increase wireless and wired internet performance. I made many changes. The first is installing a new 24-port Layer 3 networking switch to handle all of the wired and wireless clients on the home network. The second is installing a MikroTik routerboard router to handle DHCP and DNS requests for the network. The third and final change is installing a QNAP Network Attached Storage appliace that houses family photos, videos, and computer backups. A photo of the setup can be found on Slide 4.
