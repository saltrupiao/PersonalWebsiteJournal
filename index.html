About Sal:

Hi there! My name is Salvatore Trupiano. You can call me Sal for short. I am an IT major in the school of Engineering and Computer Science at Oakland University. I am a dedicated student and a hard worker. I currently work as an IT specialist at two private schools, and provide IT services at two other companies. I am currently enrolled in four courses here at Oakland University. Along with learning technology, I am also dedicated to learning anything and everything that is presented to me, in other words, I love both my engineering classes and my humanities classes! My end goal is to be an experienced IT professional. I wish to one day be an IT director for a small or even a large company. While I do not get involved heavily at OU with clubs, I do plan on joining the CyberOU club as well as maybe the robotics club in the future. I like to work even when I'm not required to be on site, its more of a fun activity to me than actual work. In my free time, I enjoy keeping up to date with the latest technology and political news, tinkering with networking equipment and learning new things, as much as I can on a daily basis.
